Product Note

Business Context

The CHSL MVP 1.001 addresses time lag and inefficiencies in accounting workflows across three key roles: Society Office Clerical Staff, Backend Accounting Vendors, and MC Members. The objective is to streamline processes, reduce delays, and improve clarity in day‑to‑day financial operations.

Role‑Specific Challenges

Clerical staff often face cumbersome, error‑prone processes. Backend accounting vendors depend on timely and accurate data entry. MC Members require transparency and up‑to‑date financial visibility. The MVP focuses on reducing friction across all three.

UX/UI Focus

The MVP prioritizes usability for clerical staff through an intuitive interface, reduced cognitive load, and “idiot‑proofing” measures such as guided prompts, validations, and clear error messages.

Testing Procedures

Testers should validate:
• Master data accuracy before voucher creation
• Voucher creation across vendors, expense heads, and amounts
• Cheque cancellation flow for compliance and reliability

Evaluation Metrics

Evaluation should focus on clerical‑staff usability:
• Speed of navigation between tasks
• Reduction and simplification of steps
• Clarity of workflows, prompts, and error handling
